About This Project

This career guidance platform was created to help high school students make informed decisions about their future careers.

Project Goals:

  • Provide accessible career guidance for students
  • Help discover careers based on individual strengths
  • Offer insights into growing career fields
  • Support bilingual students (English & Russian)

The test uses a comprehensive algorithm that evaluates your responses across multiple dimensions: interests, work style, skills, and values.
