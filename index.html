About This Project

This career guidance platform was created to help high school students make informed decisions about their future careers using evidence-based psychological assessment methods.

Project Goals:

  • Provide accessible, scientific career guidance for students
  • Help discover careers based on individual strengths and preferences
  • Offer insights into growing career fields with real salary data
  • Support bilingual students (English & Russian)
  • Make career exploration engaging and user-friendly

Scientific Foundation:

The test uses a comprehensive algorithm based on the Holland Code (RIASEC) theory, which is the most widely used career assessment model in the world. It evaluates your responses across multiple dimensions:

  • Realistic - Hands-on, practical work
  • Investigative - Analytical, research-oriented
  • Artistic - Creative, expressive
  • Social - Helping others, interpersonal
  • Enterprising - Leadership, business-oriented
  • Conventional - Organized, detail-oriented

Our algorithm maps these dimensions to four major career pathways: Technology, Healthcare, Creative, and Business.
